# odin-recipes
This project centers on the development of a website containing information about the town of El Valle del Espíritu Santo, located on Margarita Island. This picturesque town—steeped in history and culture, the birthplace of the Venezuelan national hero General Santiago Mariño, and renowned for its hospitality—is home to the beautiful and revered Virgin of the Valley, Patroness of Eastern Venezuela, the Venezuelan Navy, and all sailors.     

Through this project, I hope to further develop my web development skills—specifically my proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
